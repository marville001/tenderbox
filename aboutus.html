Tender Box






Tender Box is a highly automated I-tender and bid system, whiche aids in tender specification, advertising, tender aggregation as well as the evaluation and placing of the contract can be prepared at ease and will realize better cost savings and increase efficiency.
Tender Box gives access to the best suppliers of all times, suppliers who would not exploit or take advantage of any of the organizations whether new or old.
Tender Box is aimed to award the tenders faster in the most efficient way possible without inconveniencing any party as the system creates that open platform where deals and payments are done openly to avoid biasing or exploitation of any party.
The automation of this procurement service would be faster, safe and very efficient.
